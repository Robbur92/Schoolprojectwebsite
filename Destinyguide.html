Destiny

Destiny is a first-person shooter game developed by Bungie. Bungie also created famous shooters like the Halo series. Destiny was released on September 9th for Playstation 4, Xbox One, Playstation 3 and Xbox 360.

Classes

Titan

The Titan is the most tanky class in Destiny, with both defensive and offensive spells. The titan class is a slow hybrid class with the ability to cast defensive spells stopping all damage done against him. The titan can also chose to go head first into the enemies with a big blast. If you choose the titan you can pick between two subclasses, one is called Striker, and the other Defender. The Striker subclass is based on dealing damage to the enemy, whilst the Defender subclass is mostly used in raids and strikes to get better protection from the enemy. The titan class has 6 different types of grenades and 2 melee attacks, divided in 2 for each subclass. The titan also has 1 super ability in the Striker subclass and 1 in the Defender subclass. The Striker subclass is a lethal super where the titan smashes into the ground obliterating everything around him. The Defender super is a big shield that covers the titan and blocks all incoming damage and any other projectile.


Hunter

The Hunter is the fastest and most agile class in Destiny, this class' subclasses are both lethal subclasses designed to do immense damage. The Hunter has the potential to get a lot of agility making this class the fastest and is also the only class with double and triple jumps, while the other classes only have the ability to jump and glide. Hunter's in Destiny have two different subclasses called Gunslinger and Bladedancer. Gunslinger is a subclass where the hunter can spawn a golden gun upon using his super ability which kills anything in a single bullet. The Bladedancer super gives the Hunter immense speed and and meele distance boost for his knife attacks, giving him the ability to one shit several enemies with his knife before the super runs out. This class also has 6 different types og grenades and he can throw knifes and charge someone with a lethal knife attack.


Warlock

The Warlock is the magician of the three classes with his massive amount of AOE damage (Area of effect). This class has two subclasses where one is designed to deal massive damage in a short period of time while the other is designed to deal moderate damage over a longer period. The Warlock is faster than the Titan but slower than the Hunter. Warlocks have two subclasses, one is called Voidwalker and the other Sunsinger. The Voidwalker class has the super ability to cast a gigantic bomb to an area dealing massive damage. The Sunsinger super ability can revive the Warlock if he's dead and can also speed up the generation of grenades and magic "melee" attacks. Warlocks have 6 different types of grenades and 2 types of magical "melee" attakcs.





Planets and Locations

The Tower

The tower is the place where the Guardians live, guardians are the players of the game who are there to protect Earth and mankind. The tower consists of vendors and bank vaults, as well as bounty trackers and mission givers.


Earth

Earth is the starting point of the game, this is where the first quest is. Earth has a lot of missions and a couple of strikes aswell as patrol missions that the player can play through. Earth is also the lowest level place out of all the locations.


Moon

The Moon is the second location you get to, it's a place for the players to advance in the storyline of the game. The Moon has missions and strikes aswell as patrol missions. The Moon is the next step after completing all the missions on Earth.


Venus

Venus is the third location you get to, this planet has it all. Venus is full of missions, strikes, patrol missions aswell as the first and so far only raid in Destiny. Venus is the next step after completing all missions on the Moon.


Mars

Mars is the fourth location you get to and also consists of missions, strikes and patrol missions. Mars is the next step after completing all the missions on Venus.


The Rift

The Rift is still not unlocked for exploration. The Rift is only available in a part of a story line mission where you get to visit the Queen who lives there to advance in a mission.


Sparrows and Ships

Sparrows

The sparrow is a guardians vehicle, the sparrows is used on the different planets and locations to move around quickly. The sparrow can be upgraded for better speed and durability and can also be dropped by a boss in the Raid. The sparrow is a motorcycle looking vehicle that hovers above the ground and has a jet engine like motor on the rear for speed.


Ships

Ships are used by the guardians to travel from one location to another. The ships in the game can not be manually used by the players, but is more of an eye catcher while the game loads when travelling. You start out with a cheap ship and can get better ships by finding them in chests on planets or by drops when you kill different types of enemies.

Vendors

Agent of the Nine

Agent of the nine AKA Xûr, is a guy that sells exotic weapons and armor for strange coins and mote of light. exotic gear is the rarest gear in the game and Xûr only appears in the weekends. Strange coins and Mote of light can be obtained by killing mobs, completing bounties and delivering engrams.


Bounty Tracker

The bounty tracker gives you bounties with different tasks to complete. Once completed the bounties give you experience and reputation in different factions in the game. Bounties are also a good way to upgrade your weapons by completing a bunch of them and delivering them for massive amounts of xp to your weapons.


Crucible Vendors

The crucible vendors let's you buy gear and weapons from marks earned in the pvp part of the game. You get marks by completing online matches vs other players, theese can be spent on equipment and weapons. To be able to buy gear and weapons from theese vendors you need reputation lvl 2 and 3, lvl 2 let's you buy armor and lvl 3 let's you buy weapons.


Vanguard Vendors

The Vanguard vendor's are similair to the crucible vendors. The same rules about reputation applies to theese vendors, and you get vanguard marks by completing strikes and raids.


Gunsmith

The gunsmith let's you buy "notsogood" weapons if you do not allready have better weapons. You can also buy ammo synthesiz from him that let's you refill your ammo on missions, strikes etc.


The Postmaster

The name says it all, this guy let's you pick up your post


Materials

Ascendant Materials

There are two types of ascendant materials, the first one is ascendant shards, the other ascendant energy. Ascendant shards are used to upgrade your legendary and exotic armor pieces. The ascendant energy is used to upgrade your legendary and exotic weapons.


Dismantle materials

Each class has their own dismantle material, warlocks have hadronic essence, titan has platesteel plating and hunters have sapphire wire. These materials can be used in combination of ascendant shards to upgrade your armor.
There is also a material you get from weapons when you dismantle them, theese are called weapon parts and can be used in combination of ascendant energy to upgrade your weapons.


Resources

Resources are materials you can gather on the different planets and locations.

  • Helium Filaments (Moon)
  • Relic Iron (Mars)
  • Spirit Bloom (Venus)
  • Spinmetal (Earth)

Theese materials are needed in combination of weapon parts and ascendant energy to upgrade your weapons


Raids

Vault of Glass

Vault of Glass is the first and only Raid in Destiny so far. The raid consists of 3 bosses, The Templar, The Gatekeeper and Atheon. All of the bosses drop high end weapons and gear allowing players to reach the maximum level of 30. There will be a new raid coming in the new expansion of the game. This expansion will be released 09.12.2014.


Strikes

Strikes

Strike is a gamemode the guardians can join for a challenge vs the enemies of Destiny. Strikes consists of maps chosen from all of the planets and locations in Destiny, and is used with a matchmaking system. Allowing solo players to join up with others to complete the Strike. Strikes do not give very good rewards but are a good way of gathering glimmer (money in destiny) and vanguard marks.


Crucible

The Crucible

The crucible consists of 4 main different PvP (player vs player) based game modes

  • Clash
  • Control
  • Rumble
  • Skirmish

Clash

Clash is a 6v6 match where each team tries to get the most kills. The team with most kills when the timer runs out wins, or when one of the teams reach the score limit

Control

Control is a 6v6 match where each team tries to capture the most flags. There are 3 flags on the map, flag A, flag B and flag C. The team with the highest score when the time runs out or a score of 20 000 wins the game.

Rumble

Rumble is a free for all game where you match yourself up against 5 other players. The first player to reach a score of 2 500 wins the game or the one with the highest score when the time runs out.

Skirmish

Skirmish is a 3v3 team based matchup where the gamemode is team deathmatch. The team with the highest score wins the game
